Born in Minnesota, I moved to South Dakota at the age of 6. Here I met my wife in high school and 4 years later we welcomed our first of four children. Now, we have two sons, Cale (6 years of age) and Keaton (2 Months of age), and two daughters, Anna (4 years of age) and Emery (2 years of age).

The Big Sioux river goes through town and has carved a chanel through the quartzite rock that Dell Rapids sites on. These channels are called dells and is where Dell Rapids got its name from. It is a beautiful town and we have enjoyed living here.
